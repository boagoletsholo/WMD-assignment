Discover Botswana's Wild Wonders

Unforgettable landscapes, wildlife encounters & natural marvels

Okavango Delta

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, the Okavango Delta is a shimmering oasis of crystal-clear channels, floodplains, and islands. Explore by mokoro (dugout canoe) and spot hippos, elephants, crocodiles, and the elusive sitatunga antelope. The delta transforms with seasonal floods, creating one of Africa's greatest wildlife spectacles.

Best time: June to October (dry season).

Chobe National Park

Renowned for the largest concentration of African elephants, Chobe offers spectacular boat safaris along the Chobe River. Watch giant herds gather at dusk, see buffalo, lions, leopards, and over 450 bird species. The Savuti Marsh area is famous for predator-prey action.

Highlight: Sunset river cruises.

Makgadikgadi Pans

One of the largest salt flats in the world, a surreal white landscape stretching to the horizon. During the wet season, the pans transform into a haven for flamingos and migratory birds. Witness the second-largest zebra migration, and sleep under the stars on the shimmering salt crust.

Unique experience: Quad biking on the pans.
